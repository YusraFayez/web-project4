14 amazing 24-hour cafés in Riyadh

Sociable

CAFES

Looking for new cafés in Riyadh to socialise? With a new like Sociable, this Ar Rabi spot should fit the request.
Catch up with serves hot drinks like cortados, espresso shots, flat whites, and more.
Its signature drink, Spanish latte, and a regular latte are served both hot and cold.
Have a sweet tooth? Try Sociable’s rocky road, chocolate babka, date cake, and toffee pudding. Getting hungry?

A 12

CAFES

With the slogan “grab and fly”, A12 is all about getting you what you need and quickly at that.
Items on the cold drinks menu include iced Americano, iced Spanish latte and an iced coconut drink.
After a hot coffee? Have a flat white, espresso or latte.
And if you want to dine in, this is one of the welcoming 24-hour cafés in Riyadh.
Grab a pastry and a coffee, and settle in.

Average Café

CAFES

Fans of this café likely wouldn’t call it average.
The baristas at AV (as it’s often referred to) promise to create unique coffee drinks.
Hungry? Pancakes or waffles provide the perfect midday (or midnight) feast.

Capri Café

CAFES

Wondering what are the top 24-hour cafés in Riyadh?
Capri Café is a popular hangout spot, serving French toast, croissants, pancakes and more
whenever you like. Wondering about coffee? (Of course you are.)
The eatery offers cold brews, caramel lattes, and the Capri special.

Colpo

CAFES

If you’re looking for a fun evening hangout spot with your friends, you can check out Colpo.
It has a marble-themed interior with loud music that keeps the energy up as much as the caffeinated drinks.
And if you’re after one of the 24-hour cafés in Riyadh to begin your day, Colpo is open.

Fabrica de Café

CAFES

Want to sip coffee in a traditional setting? Out of all the 24-hour cafés in Riyadh
Fabrica de Café could be your spot.
Don’t be fooled by the name though, this spot has some serious Saudi cultural pieces.
Sample everything from a coconut latte to V60 speciality coffee.

Gourmet Café

CAFES

This place shouldn’t disappoint. Serving sandwiches, cakes and coffee
Gourmet Café has you covered with just about everything you might need pre- or post-all-nighter.
Coffee choices include V60 and a caramel macchiato.
And if you’re looking for something healthier, give the date smoothie a go.

Hampila Caffé

CAFES

Have dietary restrictions? Hampila Caffé should have you covered, and will be able to cater to you at all hours of the day and night
since it’s one of the 24-hour cafés in Riyadh.
The eatery whips up drinks with lactose-free and vegan milk options.

SORS

CAFES

Need some sugar to get you through the day or night?
SORS is known for its French toast, crème brûlée and dulce de leche cakes.
It’s one of the 24-hour cafés in Riyadh that serves classics like flat whites
Spanish lattes, and a coffee of the day.

The Palm Coffee Bar

CAFES

Made in Khobar, The Palm Coffee Bar has made its way to Riyadh to join other popular
24-hour cafés in Riyadh. The Instagrammable eatery is decorated with coconut-shaped seats
and a tree in the very centre of the seating area.
And you can even drink coffee out of a coconut, if you order the iced palm special.
Want something besides coconut? Iced Americanos and iced shaken drinks are on the menu, too

Third

CAFES

Coffee from all over the world? Count us in.
Third serves coffee beans from Ethiopia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, and more.
If you’re a coffee nerd, this is one of the 24-hour cafés that should deliver with its variety of hot and cold drinks.

Sentio Café

CAFES

Looking for summery vibes along with your coffee?
The search could end here. Sentio Café greets you
with brightly coloured flowers, a striking outdoor seating area
and tonnes of ice cream and dessert options including fudge brownies
French toast brûlée, cookies, and more. If you have a massive sweet tooth,
this is one of the 24-hour cafés in Riyadh for you.

Stranger Co

CAFES

This fun café comes up with a new special every month
and the creative takes are worth coming back for alone.
Stranger Co has dreamed up everything from Vimto coffee to cheesecake lattes as specials.
Having turned three in February, this place is keeping it original and showing no sign of running
out of ideas. More of a traditionalist?
Enjoy core coffee classics, too. Spanish latte, anyone?

Jolt

CAFES

Fittingly, Jolt aims to provide caffeine to Riyadh’s residents.
The dimly lit ambience is a welcoming space at all hours of the day and night
whether you’re looking to study, work or catch up with friends.
